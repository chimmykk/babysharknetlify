Features

Dynamic Buyback

The dynamic buyback allows for longer, more organic and sustainable pumps. Moreover, the dynamic buyback acts as a deterrent for huge sells. Whale dumps are punished not only by the dynamic fee but also by the buyback.

Antibot

Various anti-bot measures are in place in $GSHARK to prevent abuse by bots, such as a 15 seconds cooldown between each buy and sell. Frontrun bots have no power here.

Static Rewards

A portion (2%) is cut on each transaction which awards every user with free tokens in their wallets. as people conduct transactions. Hodlers are effectively paid for merely holding $BSHARK in their wallets.
